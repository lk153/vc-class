Toast: Stacked notifications
Stacked toast notifications that use 3D-style transforms and opacity to push back into a visual stack, with AnimatePresence for enter/exit.

Reference https://motion.dev/examples/react-toast-stack

Setting Options:
Toast Stack:
    - Stack Offset Y: 13
    - Stack Scale: 0.06
    - Stack Opacity: 0.20
    - Stagger Interval: 0.05
    - Toast Spring:
      - Type: Physics
        - Stiffness: 400
        - Damping: 30
        - Mass: 1.0
